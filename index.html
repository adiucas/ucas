About UCAS

United Corporate Advisory Services (UCAS) Limited was formed on November 22, 2009 and started its operation from January 2010. The company has expertise in the field of software solution, data Processing and IT Enabled Service (ITES). Please be informed that UCAS has core business expertise in the field of IT enabled Services (ITES) with focus on various wings of Capital Market capital market related back office software services. We also have the expertise on Holding AGM on virtual Platform Post Issue Management of IPO, data processing related to Rights Share Offer, Bonus and Dividend, Share Management software solution etc.

Mission

‘Clients’ Service First’ is our mission and motto to achieve excellence. We visualize the path to understand our customers’ business goal and analyze the needs to achieve it. We design software solution accordingly. Our goal is to achieve excellence in quality products and services through continuous improvement and innovation. With this, we aspire to forge mutually beneficial and long lasting relationship with clients. We strive for services that enable our clients to exceed excellence in their business goals and challenges through effective use of Software and ITES solutions and expertise.

Vision

United Corporate Advisory Services (UCAS) Limited was formed with focus and aspiration to become market leader in the field of software solution and ITES provider related to various wings of capital market of the country. With firm commitment to quality and satisfy our clients, we always try to remain a center of excellence in ICT services. Our ultimate vision is to be a model of excellence as software solution and an IT enabled Services (ITES) Provider Company that our Customers and Associates would love to be associated with.
