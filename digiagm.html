background of UCAS Digital-AGM/EGM

Bangladesh Security Exchange Commission has directed all the publicly listed companies to conduct AGM/EGM and Board of Directors' Meeting through Hybrid System (a combination of physical & digital platform) vide their order no. BSEC/CMRRCD/2009-193/08 dated 10 March 2021 and BSEC/CMRRCD/2009-193/12 dated 23 March 2021. We understand that in compliance with the said order you are going to conduct your next Annual General Meeting in digital Platform. We are rendering the technical support to you for the last few years to conduct Annual General Meetings and dividend data processing successfully and in compliant manner. On that continuation we want to have the opportunity to serve you in the newly launched digital platform as suggested by Bangladesh Securities and Exchange Commission. We have developed required technical expertise to comply with this new phenomenon. You are our valued client for several years and we are maintaining all the data confidentiality all these years. We have termed our newly developed digital platform as “UCAS Digital-AGM/EGM”.


“UCAS Digital-AGM/EGM” has been designed and developed by the technical team of United Corporate Advisory Services (UCAS) Limited on the light of knowledge acquired for over a decade by our technical core team. We have hand on experience in serving the regulations and technical expertise to meet all the compliance matter required for any publicly listed company in the stock market of the country. Our main focus for all these years have is capital market and we provide ITES technical support, corporate advisory, consultation on issues related to capital market from our inception in 2009.
