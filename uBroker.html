Main Modules/Features of the UBroker

Data conversion from Existing or previous system. Historical data will be available in the new system.
Trader or employee performance analysis facility.
Dealer & broker both has same interface & has the facility to login from one to another system
Regulatory Reports are available.
Multi User Access module with report level accessing.
Email facility for sending Portfolio, trade confirmation, DSE News, research reports & other
reports. Has the facility for sending general email like greetings.
SMS facility for sending trade confirmation & any other SMS.
Bulk IPO application system.
Integrated Accounting System.
RTGS & BEFTN Facility for monetary transaction.
